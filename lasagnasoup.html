Lasagna soup

Viral tiktok "lasagna soup"

prep time

10 mins

cook time

20mins

serving

2 servings

Lasagna Soup

instructions

step 1

  1. Cook chopped onion and garlic in some olive oil
  2. Add your beef
  3. Mix with olive oil
  4. Season with Salt, black pepper , chilli flakes, oregano and mix
  5. Add tomato paste then 4 cups of beef broth ( or just water) and cook for 5 mins
  6. Add tomato sauce
  7. Break the lasagna sheets however you like then add them
  8. Add the heavy cream
  9. Cook for 15 mins then add your cheese on top

ingredients

1 small onion

2 cloves of garlic

1/2 lb ground beef

1/2 heavy cream

1 tbsp tomato paste

1 can of tomato sauce

8 Lasagna sheets

Mozzarella cheese
