Vegan Falafel

Healthy Vegan falafel recipe with lentils and chickpea

prep time

90 mins

cook time

20mins

serving

4 servings

Vegan Healthy

instructions

Preparation

  1. Soak red lentils in water for an hour
  2. Chop onion and parsley into little cubes
  3. Blend the onion, parsley and garlic until chunky
  4. Add the lentils
  5. Blend for 5 mins
  6. Add the seasoning
  7. Add the chickpea flour and stir it in
  8. Shape into falafel and fry in oil or bake at 200C for 20mins

ingredients

1 cup red lentils

Onions

parsley

2 cloves of Garlic

4 tbsp Chickpea flour

1 tsp each cumin , ground coriander and salt
