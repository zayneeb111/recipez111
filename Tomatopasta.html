Tomato pasta

prep time

10 mins

cook time

15mins

serving

2 servings

Pasta

instructions

step 1 : Pasta

  1. Put water to boil for 5 mins
  2. Add your pasta
  3. Let cook for 10mins

step 2 : Sauce

  1. Add your butter till melted
  2. Add chopped onion and garlic saute till they shrink in size a bit
  3. Add Chopped tomatoes and let saut√©
  4. Add 2 tbsp of tomato paste
  5. season with salt,pepper,red chilli flakes and chilli powder
  6. Add 1/3 cup pasta water
  7. Sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and mozzarella
  8. Mix in your pasta

ingredients

Pasta

3 tbsp butter

onion and 3 minced garlics

1 tsp each seasoning

2 tbsp tomato paste

Parmesan Cheese and Mozzarella

Two tomatoes or cherry tomatoes
