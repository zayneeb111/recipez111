Veggies rice

Healthy veggies Rice with Creamy sauce

prep time

15 mins

cook time

30mins.

serving

2 servings

Vegetables Healthy Rice

instructions

step 1 : Veggies

  1. Cut any Available Veggies same size (not too small )
  2. Season with Salt, black pepper , chilli flakes, paprika
  3. Mix with olive oil
  4. Roast in the oven for 20/30 mins (keep checking) on 180Â°

step 2 : Cooking Rice

  1. Wash rice 2 times
  2. Add 1.5 cup of water
  3. Bring to simmer on medium high for 4mins
  4. Turn down to low and Cover with lid
  5. Cook for 15/20mins

step 3 : Sauce

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan
  2. Stir in the flour for 1 min
  3. Gradually stir in the milk
  4. Keep stirring constantly
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Add your cheese
  7. Reduce the heat and simmer for 5 mins

ingredients

Any amount of vegtables you'd like

1 cup of Rice

1 Spoon of butter

1 Spoon of flour

1/4 L of milk

Cheese
